# Bank Bank Lead AI Engineer

Role definition for NN Bank

Audience: Executives and Leadership
Source: _local-research-001

---

## Why this role

AI delivery needs five disciplines to work together:

- Data Science
- Software Engineering
- Architecture
- Business Analysis
- Controls

We miss a role connecting all five.

---

## The gap

```
Data Science      ->  models without production rigor
Engineering       ->  systems without AI depth
Architecture      ->  integration without AI constraints
Business Analysis ->  requirements without feasibility
Controls          ->  frameworks without AI specificity
```

Each discipline optimizes locally as they are already complex enough.

---

## The proposal

A **Bank Lead AI Engineer** as a **connective (glue) role**

- Reports through the **CTO** organization
- **Advisory authority** across all BUs and platform teams
- Owns no team, influences every team

---

## Six responsibility domains

| Domain | Focus |
|---|---|
| Agentic for Business | Agent solutions for BU processes |
| Agentic for Coding | AI-assisted dev tools and standards |
| Agentic for Architecture | Reference architectures for AI |
| Agentic for Engineering | MLOps, day-two, production rigor |
| Agentic for Governance | Automated quality gates |
| AI Controls Advisory | AI-specific risk controls with Controls dept |

---

## Lifecycle ownership

```
+------------+    +------------+    +--------------+
| Conception |--->|  Delivery  |--->|  Operations  |
+------------+    +------------+    +--------------+
      ^                                    |
      +----- Feedback & lessons ----------+

      Bank Lead AI Engineer: advisory across all three
```

Day-two (monitoring, drift, retraining) is non-negotiable in financial services.

---

## Organizational positioning

Three models evaluated:

- **A. Embedded** in one team -> too narrow
- **B. Advisory** across all teams -> **selected**
- **C. Dedicated AI team** -> premature for current scale

---

## Why advisory (Model B)

| Criterion | Result |
|---|---|
| Influence breadth | High |
| Fit with NN Bank structure | Strong |
| Hiring feasibility | Hard (wide+deep profile) |
| Accountability clarity | Lower - mitigated by quality gates |
| Execution speed | Lower - mitigated by champions |

Plan a transition to a small team when 3+ BUs run AI projects simultaneously.

---

## Required profile

**Depth**

- 5+ years AI/ML in production
- LLM patterns: RAG, fine-tuning, evaluation
- MLOps, cloud, distributed systems

**Breadth**

- Software architecture and system design
- Financial services domain
- Cross-functional leadership without direct authority

---

## Key risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Influence without teeth | Mandatory quality gates, CTO sponsorship |
| Single point of failure | Champions network, codified artifacts |
| Scope overload | Tiered engagement, ruthless prioritization |
| Territory friction | Position as enabling, not replacing |
| Advisory lag | Mandatory inception involvement |

---

## Enabling structures

- **AI Champions Network** - one per active team
- **Tiered engagement** - Deep / Light / Self-service
- **Architecture board seat** - early visibility
- **Virtual AI Practice Group** - cross-team forum
- **Knowledge infrastructure** - reusable artifacts
- **CTO-backed operating rhythm** - protects strategy time

---

## Tiered engagement

| Tier | When | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Deep | New AI projects through first release | Weekly pairing |
| Light | Running AI systems | Periodic, ad-hoc |
| Self-service | Using published standards | None |

Prevents the role from spreading equally thin.

---

## Success measures

- AI projects pass quality gates without rework
- Controls are **AI-specific** and adopted
- Cross-BU **standards** in active use
- Team **self-sufficiency** grows over time
- Time-to-production for new AI projects decreases

Measured by organizational capability, not individual delivery.

---

## What we ask

- Charter the role under the CTO
- Mandate involvement at AI project inception
- Authorize quality-gate sign-off
- Sponsor escalation when standards are bypassed

---

## Open questions

- Formal reporting line and escalation path
- Progression path when things grow more

To be resolved in subsequent BDRs.
